Effects of dietary flavonoids on performance, blood constituents, carcass composition and small intestinal morphology of broilers: a meta-analysis. (Special Issue: Immune, herbs and systems for monogastric animals.)

Prihambodo, T. R. and Sholikin, M. M. and Novia, Qomariyah and Anuraga, Jayanegara and Irmanida, Batubara and Utomo, D. B. and Nahrowi, Nahrowi

Animal Bioscience (2021) 34: 434–442

DOI: 10.5713/ajas.20.0379

Abstract

Objective: This study aims to evaluate the influence of dietary flavonoids on the growth performance, blood and intestinal profiles, and carcass characteristics of broilers by employing a meta-analysis method.
