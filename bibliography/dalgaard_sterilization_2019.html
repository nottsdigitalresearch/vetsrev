Sterilization, pain and convalescence. [Danish]

Dalgaard, T. T.

Dansk Veterinaertidsskrift (2019) 102: 32–39

Link: https://dvt.ddd.dk/bladarkiv/2019/nr-08/sterilisering-smerte-og-rekonvalescens/

Abstract

Do bitches, neutralized with laparoscopy, feel less pain and have a better recovery than bitches neutralized with open midline laparotomy.
