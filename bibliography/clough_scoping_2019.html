A scoping review of the current literature exploring the nature of the horse-human relationship

Clough, H. G. R. and Burford, J. H. and Roshier, A. L. and England, G. C. W. and Freeman, S. L.

Veterinary Evidence (2019) 4:

DOI: 10.18849/VE.V4I4.240

Abstract

Objective: To perform a scoping review of the current evidence on the horse-human relationship. Background: The horse-human relationship has a significant impact on how horse owners care for and make decisions for their horse. Evidentiary value: Identification of consensus and gaps in current evidence.
