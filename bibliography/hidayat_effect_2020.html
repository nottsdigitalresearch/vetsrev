Effect of zinc on the immune response and production performance of broilers: a meta-analysis

Hidayat, C. and Sumiati and Jayanegara, A. and Wina, E.

Asian Australasian Journal of Animal Sciences (2020) 33: 465–479

DOI: 10.5713/ajas.19.0146

Abstract

Objective: This study performed a meta-analysis of published trials to determine the effects of zinc on the immune response and production performance of broilers.
